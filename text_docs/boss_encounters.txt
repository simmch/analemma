Ssarik
A general in the Zaltys Serpents Embrace army. He invaded the dwarves ruins and potentially killed Varok for revenge since Varok infiltrated Serpents Embrace as a spy and killed Ssaik’s older brother.

Varok 
A Whisper general. He infiltrated Serpents Embrace and managed to kill Ssaik’s older brother. He nearly killed No Beard Gog and I. We managed to rescue Aero and his dog. Varok is Aero's uncle.
