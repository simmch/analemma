Ring of Shadow (Vexra)
Appearance: A matte black ring that seems to absorb light.
Power: Grants control over shadows, allowing the wielder to cast darkness at will and manipulate shadowy forms.

Ring of Soul (Lunarion) [Corrupted]
Appearance: Originally prismatic, now tainted with dark corruption.
Power: Controls and manipulates souls, with the ability to reap or repair them. Can conjure a pact weapon at will.

Ring of Stone (King Durin)
Appearance: Crafted from unyielding stone, etched with ancient Dwarven runes. Inner green glow covered by rough cuts of rock and stone 
Power: Mastery over metal, stone and earth, granting abilities like petrification, earth manipulation, and creating constructs.
